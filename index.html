Gastro Bar Saimaa - a restaurant by students for everyone

By Linh My Tong

2 min read


Our restaurant offers fresh seasonal products with the best quality and reasonable price. We are welcome you to come and have a great experience with us.


You want to have a fancy meal but your budget will not allow you? You want to enjoy dishes from all around the world but there is no restaurant near you can offer that? Then, Gastro Bar Saimaa is the perfect place for you.


What is Gastro Bar Saimaa?


Gastro Bar Saimaa is a restaurant managed by Jukka and Ann Marie, combined with all the students who study Tourism and Hospitality management in LAB University of Applied Sciences. All the dishes are designed, cooked, and served by the students for everyone who wishes to come and try them. Gastro Bar Saimaa organizes themed events where customers can try various dishes from different cuisines in a welcoming themed atmosphere . We guarantee to create the most authentic international dishes with our fresh ingredients.


Our inspirations and motives


Customer satisfaction is the greatest motivation for us to overcome new challenges, new taste bud and bringing you the best we have. By having international students work together in a friendly environment, our inspirations was and still is to create the best dishes from all around the world for every holiday's seasons. Combine with seeing our customers are happy with the food, it is our motivation to keep on creating and serving you with high quality dishes.



Offers and Where to find us


From a small town in the North of Finland, Gastro Bar Saimaa, which located in Lappeenranta promises you great offers such as discount code, buy one get one desert for free etc..., You can visit us on Facebook or Instagram to follow our menu and even get a chance for amazing gifts including coupons, discounts and free meal by using hashtag such as #gastrobarsaimaa with a picture of your dish. One easy step for such great offers.



We are looking foward to see you!
